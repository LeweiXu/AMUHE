## TRANSLATION DECISIONS
 
- 少女の望まぬ英雄譚 (C.241 title) = "A Maiden's Unwanted Heroic Epic" (matches series title; the chapter title is the series title itself, used as the climactic revelation of its meaning — the heroic epic Krische never wanted, praised by soldiers while she lost everything)
- 大海の蛙 (C.242 title) = "The Frog in the Great Sea" (大海 = great sea/ocean, 蛙 = frog; idiom 大海の蛙 inverts the proverb 井の中の蛙 "frog in a well" — Selene feels herself to be a tiny frog lost in a vast ocean, overwhelmed by the scale of Krische/Bery/Kreschenta's world)
- 永遠の雛鳥 (C.243 title) = "The Eternal Nestling" (永遠 = eternal, 雛鳥 = nestling/fledgling; Krische as a chick that will forever chase its first imprint — Bery — and never leave the nest)
- 真名 (C.244 title) = "True Name" (真名 = true name/hidden name; refers to the secret noble birth name "" and its role as the key to finding Bery's soul)
- 光輝醜彩 (C.245 title) = "Radiance and Ruin" (光輝 = radiance/brilliance, 醜彩 = ugly coloring; a coined compound — the beautiful light of Bery's soul vs. the ugliness Selene perceives in her own. Kept as two-word title capturing the contrast)
- 雷槍 = "lightning spear" (雷 = lightning/thunder, 槍 = spear; Krische's mana-constructed weapon in C.241)
- 大光槍 = "great light-spear" (大 = great, 光 = light, 槍 = spear; variant name for the same weapon)
- 霊体 = "spirit-body" (霊 = spirit/soul, 体 = body; the soul's form when separated from the physical body)
- 霊質 = "soul-substance" (霊 = spirit, 質 = substance/matter; the material composing the soul)
- 空間転移 = "spatial transference" (空間 = space, 転移 = transference/teleportation)
- 根源 = "the source" (previously established; confirmed as a massive sphere where all souls and mana converge and cycle)
- 停止の術式 = "time-stop formulae" (停止 = stop/halt, 術式 = formula/spell-formula)
- ヴェズレア軍 = "Vezrea army" (Red's rebel force)
