## TRANSLATION DECISIONS

- 陰影彩美 (C.246 title) = "Shadows and Splendor" (陰影 = shadow/shading, 彩美 = colorful beauty; the chapter contrasts the shadows in Selene's self-image with the beauty she perceives in others' souls)
- 憧憬無垢 (C.247 title) = "Longing and Innocence" (憧憬 = longing/yearning, 無垢 = innocence/purity; Selene's longing set against Krische's unblemished purity)
- 認めないもの (C.248 title) = "What She Will Not Accept" (認めない = refuse to accept/acknowledge; refers to Kreschenta's refusal to accept Bery's death/her own feelings. Deliberately ambiguous singular "she" — applies to both Kreschenta and Krische)
- 番外編　日々年々 (C.249 title) = "Side Story: Days and Years" (日々 = days, 年々 = years; the slow accumulation of ordinary time, and its passage)
- 穢れなき園 (C.250 title) = "The Unsullied Garden" (穢れなき = without stain/unsullied, 園 = garden; the paradise Krische seeks, and the ironic weight of "unsullied" given the chapter's themes)
- 王の五指 = "King's Five Fingers" (the five major roads from Albernaria)
- 黒旗剣術指南所 = "Black Flag Swordsmanship Academy" (剣術指南所 = swordsmanship teaching hall)
- Kreschenta's use of わたくし and ですわ in C.248 when addressing Bery maintains her established register; her self-reference as "Krische" when speaking to Bery in the role of the one delivering death reflects that she is speaking as the twin/substitute.
- C.249 confirms Nora (ノーラ) already listed in context.md; no romanization change needed.
- C.250 time skip: decades after Continental Unification. Kalua, Mia, and Elvena are elderly; Krische and Kreschenta unchanged. Black Flag disbanded. Jaleia Gashea and magical weapons dominate battlefields.
