Speaks with clipped military formality in battle; more reflective and philosophical in private. Addresses enemies with grudging respect. Refuses "my lady" from her adjutant. Secretly fond of stuffed animals and children.

## SECONDARY CHARACTERS

**Milkaruz** — Teknrea Remin's adjutant. Elderly man; long-serving retainer of the Remin ducal house. Calls Teknrea "my lady" (お嬢さま) and has sworn to do so until she marries. Speech: warm, teasing, grandfatherly. Minor role (C.197–198).
**Kanalua warrior chief** — Commander of the Veze right wing. Led his elite against Kokys's Second Corps in a sacrificial charge to allow Red's retreat. Name not given; identified only by tribal title. Killed in battle. Speech: formal battlefield oratory. (C.198)
**Teknrea Remin** — (Existing entry update) Full name confirmed as テックレア＝タルク＝リネア＝レーミン (Teknrea Taluk Rinea Remin) in C.200 profile. Duke's daughter; inherited the house after her half-brother's death in his first battle. Nearly forty years old, unmarried. Fights with Rollka-style longsword and steel shield; the shield is used offensively as a bludgeon per Rollka doctrine. Visor engraved with a face of wrath. Lost a corps commander under her watch (Bez killed said commander's force, not the commander herself — the "corps commander" lost was on her own side, from the Elderant assault). Secretly collects stuffed animals. Alignment: Law/Good. Approved Felwors's handling of mopping up. Showed moral qualms about Krische's no-surrender order but lacked the standing to object.
**Shelna Veze** — (Existing entry update) Killed by Krische (C.198). Throat slit with "the light one" after a brief duel. Krische used Aron's curved sword as a thrown weapon; Shelna caught it but was outmaneuvered. Her head was displayed on a banner at the Kils main camp alongside Aron, Zren, Jiegret, and others. C.200 profile confirms her alignment as Neutral/Evil — an abnormal person who discovered empathy through Red, and whose sole desire was a peaceful life with him. Epithet: "gale" (烈風). Killed a man at age seven and was observed examining his organs, leading to her reputation as a mad princess.
**Red** — (Existing entry update) C.200 profile confirms alignment shift from Neutral/Good to Neutral/Evil following Shelna's death. Survived the battle; was found by Toba Akaz's retreating northern force. Discovered Shelna's head displayed at the Kils camp. Now consumed by despair and hatred toward Alberan.
**Bez** — (Existing entry update) Killed in battle against Teknrea Remin's forces (C.197). Fought as a death squad after sending Red back to the main camp. Teknrea acknowledged his valor, placed a sword in his hand per Elderant custom (warrior's death = departed as a heroic spirit), and personally finished him. Confirmed as Red's father's old friend and former warrior chief candidate who voluntarily lost to Red in their duel.
**Zren** — (Existing entry update) Killed by Vinsreel's griffin-borne lance strike from above (C.198). Was pierced through the shoulder while fighting one-armed. Had previously bowed to Aron Kils to secure his cooperation for Shelna's retreat. C.200 profile confirms he was Red's childhood rival, three years older, who stepped aside after realizing Shelna loved Red.
**Aron Kils** — (Existing entry update) Killed by Krische (C.197). Gururun slammed him to the ground mid-leap; Krische shot an arrow through his skull. His curved sword was taken by Krische and used against Shelna. Head displayed on a banner post-battle.
**Jiegret** — (Existing entry update) Killed by Krische during the retreat (C.197–198). Decapitated; head thrown at Shelna's position to demoralize. C.200 profile confirms he was 150 years old, a master of mana crystal inscription who crafted seven priestly staves in his lifetime, and one of the top five contemporary magic researchers.
**Bery** — (Existing entry update) C.199 confirms the intimate scene with Krische. Bery initiated physical intimacy while under the influence of alcohol; the scene is framed through metaphor (moonlight devoured by shadow, red hair covering silver). C.200 profile lists her current worry as "crossed the line" (手を出してしまった). The taste-restoration candy successfully delivered honey flavor via mana-based sensory reproduction. Krische plans to inscribe the permanent spell on Bery's tongue once fully developed.
