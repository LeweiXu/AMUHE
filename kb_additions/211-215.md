## SPEECH PATTERNS

- **Elzaldo Gokkalus:** Calm, measured, understated confidence. Economy of speech befitting a veteran general. Self-deprecating about his own talent while exhibiting sharp psychological insight. Uses moderate-formal register.
- **Lieberis:** Young, earnest, idealistic. Commands with a centurion's directness (俺). Philosophical when introspective — values individual human lives over numbers. Courageous but not reckless.
- **Ranu Karud:** Tyrannical rage. Commands through fear; kills dissenters without hesitation. Speech barely described (narrated rather than quoted), but the portrait is of a man consumed by wrath.

## SECONDARY CHARACTERS

**Elzaldo Gokkalus** — (Existing entry update) Full given name エルザルド confirmed in C.212. Central General; commands the defense against Elsren mounted archers on the southwestern plains of Wulfenite. Scarred face, one missing eye, bristling mustache (虎髭), powerfully built. Fights a purely defensive battle using prepared screens, ropes, and crossbows lent by Arna. Self-deprecating about his abilities; excels at psychological warfare — lures opponents into overconfidence by performing "just enough incompetence for the opponent to barely comprehend." Speech: calm, resonant, measured.

**Linkara** — Bazlar Lukazan's adjutant/officer. Present at the eastern command during the Wulfenite siege. Reports on supply disruptions and strategic conditions. Speech: measured, analytical.

**Minuos Natrias** — Emperor's second son (Elsren Holy Empire). Appointed by the Papal Court to command the logistical army for the holy war. Placement is political — prevents Lukazan from monopolizing war glory. Bazlar suspects him of deliberate supply sabotage. No direct appearance; referenced only.

**Lieberis** — Elsren centurion; commoner-born, rose to centurion rank in three years. Father was a nobleman who abandoned his mother. Skilled mana-user. Led a ten-squad assault on a Jaleia Gashea, correctly identifying its head as its weak point. Killed by Krische's hand-thrown Bonjara just before striking the finishing blow. Speech: direct (俺), idealistic, values individual lives. Philosophical in internal monologue.

**Ranu Karud** — Elsren great chieftain (大族長) of the mounted archers. Led roughly a thousand survivors into the Kalbyas Mountains after the defeat. Tyrannical and paranoid; killed dissenters, used the weakest soldiers to test unknown food. Killed by his own men in his sleep — shot with hundreds of arrows. His successor immediately surrendered to Alberan.

**Biinal Sarshenka** — (Existing entry update) Fled Wulfenite into the Kureyl Mountains after the battle. Found by Aleha during the mop-up sweep. Reduced to shirt and trousers, having discarded his armor. Accepted death at Aleha's hand to secure war-death benefits for his wife and daughter (Reshel). Requested that his death be recorded as a brave fall in battle. Buried by Aleha and Waltza; his sword kept for eventual delivery to his family.

**Aleha Sarshenka** — (Existing entry update) Appointed Fourth Corps Commander of the Alberinea Direct Army (announced C.214). Killed his brother Biinal in the Kureyl Mountains as a mercy — to secure death benefits for Biinal's wife and daughter. Expressed complex feelings: partial resentment, partial respect for Biinal's devotion as husband and father. Buried Biinal with Waltza's help.

**Kalua** — (Existing entry update) Present at Wulfenite during the siege cleanup (C.211). Rode Gururun with Krische atop the tower. Expressed "big sis" concern for Krische's well-being. Declined Krische's hamburger steak offer after seeing the corpse pits.

**Kokys** — (Existing entry update) Confirmed appointed/continuing as corps commander (C.214 banquet scene). Boisterous at the victory banquet; called Aleha over to drink.
