## SECONDARY CHARACTERS

**Argoush Vikel Gietelens** — Marquis; Anne Gietelens's father. Non-military nobleman but sharp administrator known as a shrewd, diligent lord. Governs the Peerls region near the Creisharana border. First to encounter Krische and Rigaleiwe upon their arrival at Pilke Fort (C.154).

## WORLD-BUILDING REFERENCE

### Yagelnaus / Rigaleiwe
- **True name:** Rigaleiwe of Albyagel (アルビャーゲルのリーガレイヴ). "Yagelnaus" is a human-bestowed honorific, not the dragon's name.
- **Dragon names:** Equivalent to human true names (真名). Used only between dragons who regard each other as equals when entering pacts. Humans are never normally permitted to use them.
- **Pact with Krische (C.152–153):** Acknowledged Krische as an equal after she demonstrated the ability to threaten its life. Granted her the right to use its true name and agreed to assist with Bery's cure.
- **Personality:** Amused rather than angered by Krische's attacks. Claims pain is "pleasant" after a thousand years without it. Finds the evolution of small beings fascinating. Views human affairs with detached interest.
- **Dragon's blood:** Potent; three drops sufficient to cure Bery. Excessive amounts lethal to humans. Descendants of those who consumed dragon flesh in antiquity have bodies more tolerant of dragon blood (implies mana-users are such descendants).
- **Sacred Convention origin (expanded):** Bazarishe personally approached Rigaleiwe and proposed the mutual non-interference pact. Bazarishe was female. Rigaleiwe remembers her as "cunning" and "arrogant" — similar to Krische but more politically shrewd.

### Magic System (additions)
- **Mana color:** Mana has an individual "color" unique to each person. Ambient mana is colorless/transparent. Using another entity's mana requires first stripping its color — a time-consuming process. Dragon-origin mana is especially difficult to convert.
- **Krische's canopy (C.152):** A geometric lens constructed mid-air from residual dragon mana, capable of reflecting the dragon's roar. Represents a new application of her crystal-free magic.
- **Mana spear (C.152):** Krische condensed captured dragon mana into a lightning-like throwing weapon — effectively a miniature dragon's roar. Used to strike Rigaleiwe from the sky.
- **Griffin flight mechanics:** Griffins fly by concentrating their mana exclusively into wind manipulation — pure brute force, not physical augmentation. Krische lacks the raw mana to replicate this.

### Historical Lore (additions)
- **Bazarishe:** Confirmed female. Described by Rigaleiwe as the most cunning human, who used the dragon itself to impose order on surrounding nations. Had purple eyes and silver hair like Krische. Proposed the Sacred Convention as a mutual non-interference pact beneficial to both sides.

## RECURRING THEMES AND MOTIFS

**Dragon's laughter** — Rigaleiwe's amusement is expressed as intermittent rippling mana waves (断続的な魔力の波), consistently described throughout C.151–155. Rendered as "intermittent mana rippled" or "mana rippled as if in laughter."

**Krische as paradox** — The 天帝矛盾 (Heavenly Sovereign Paradox) title of C.152 frames Krische as the irresistible force against the immovable dragon — an inversion of the classical spear-and-shield paradox. The Creisharana warriors experience this as simultaneous desire to kill her and refusal to see her die.
